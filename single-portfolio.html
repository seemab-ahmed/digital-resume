Project Info:

Category : Web Development
Date : 02 October, 2019
Client : Oniblue
Web : www.applanding.com

CHALLENGE

A potential high-value client was identified with an immediate need for services. Despite having no prior connection, the company aimed to get onto the client’s bid list and secure the opportunity. Complicating matters, the client engaged multiple firms during their evaluation process, creating a highly competitive environment. The client requested a pitch presentation, which Marketing leveraged into a broader engagement strategy. By creating personalized assets and touchpoints before and after the pitch, Marketing transformed a single request into a comprehensive, relationship-building opportunity.

SOLUTION

Understanding the Buyer’s Journey

• Marketing identified key personas within the target account, acknowledging that each decision-maker was at a different stage of the buyer’s journey

• Ads and content were customized to address specific needs for each persona, fostering engagement and accelerating the decision-making process.

Personalized Sales Rep Introductions

• Marketing ran ads featuring the assigned sales representative, ensuring that outreach felt familiar and warm. This approach transitioned the interaction from a cold outreach to a trusted connection.

Strategic Presentation Team Assembly

• Comprehensive research revealed detailed insights into the client’s stakeholders, including their likes, priorities, and recent developments.

• The sales team was matched with individuals who had the greatest alignment in expertise and shared interests, ensuring a relatable and credible presence

Industry Expertise Integration

• Marketing identified emerging industry trends and crafted a narrative that aligned with the client’s strategic priorities. This positioned the company as a knowledgeable and forward-thinking partner.

Creative Content Development

• Custom online experiences and dynamic presentation materials were developed to set the company apart from competitors. These creative assets left a lasting impression and reinforced the company’s commitment to innovation.

RESULTS

Bid List Success

• The company secured a spot on the client’s bid list and ultimately won the contract, demonstrating the effectiveness of a holistic ABM strategy.

Accelerated Engagement

• By addressing decision-makers’ unique needs, Marketing successfully shortened the sales cycle and deepened stakeholder engagement.

Positive Brand Perception

• The personalized touch and creative approach resonated with the client, enhancing their perception of the company as a partner invested in their success.

KEY LEARNINGS AND CONCLUSION

Agility and Adaptability

• The client’s internal changes and external economic pressures necessitated a fluid strategy. Marketing quickly adapted to these shifts, ensuring alignment with the client’s evolving needs.

The Power of Personalization

• Understanding individual stakeholders and tailoring interactions proved invaluable in building trust and securing buy-in across decision-makers.

Cross-Functional Collaboration

• Strong coordination between Marketing and Sales was instrumental in creating a seamless and impactful client experience.

The Role of Industry Insights

• Providing relevant expertise helped position the company as a strategic advisor, a critical factor in winning the account.
