CASE STUDY

ABM /  POST SALE EXPANSION

CASE STUDY

CHALLENGE

After an internal reorganization and a strategic shift, the company began experiencing declining engagement and revenue from a critical client. The company realized it needed a robust post-sales expansion strategy to demonstrate the full potential of its products and services. The goal was to help the client see how the offerings could address new challenges and opportunities, ensuring longterm loyalty and growth.

SOLUTION

Identifying Opportunities for Upsell and Cross-Sell

• By analyzing usage patterns and pain points, the team crafted targeted campaigns to highlight features and services that addressed the client’s evolving needs.

• Engagement signals, such as clicks on targeted ads and downloads of specific content, were used to identify areas of interest and initiate deeper conversations.

Personalized Content and Dynamic Landing Pages

• Marketing developed personalized landing pages that aligned with the client’s specific pain points and interests, ensuring relevance and resonance.

• Dynamic content showcased innovative use cases and solutions, helping to redefine the client’s perception of the company’s capabilities.

Targeted Account Reviews

• Regular reviews with the client provided an opportunity to analyze their usage patterns, propose tailored solutions, and address potential gaps in their implementation.

These reviews fostered open dialogue and positioned the company as a proactive partner in the client’s success.

Multichannel Engagement

• A mix of digital ads, direct mail, and virtual events ensured comprehensive engagement with key stakeholders at different levels within the client’s organization.

• Personalized experiences were designed for individuals focused on innovation, further solidifying the company’s position as a thought leader.

ABM’s Gradual Expansion

• The team adopted a “crawl, walk, run” approach to their ABM strategy, finetuning campaigns at each stage and scaling as confidence and insights grew.

• A mix of digital ads, custom reach outs, and virtual events ensured comprehensive engagement with key stakeholders at different levels within the client’s organization.

• Personalized experiences were designed for individuals focused on innovation, further solidifying the company’s position as a thought leader.

RESULTS

Revenue Recovery

• The targeted ABM efforts led to a significant increase in revenue from the account, reversing the earlier decline.

Stronger Client Relationships

• Regular account reviews and personalized outreach deepened trust and strengthened the partnership.

Higher Engagement Rates

• Personalized landing pages and content led to increased engagement metrics, including higher click-through rates and deeper content consumption.

KEY LEARNINGS AND CONCLUSION

Gradual Scaling Ensures Success

• Starting small and scaling gradually allows for continuous learning and optimization, resulting in more effective and impactful campaigns.

Personalization Drives Results

• Personalized content and targeted outreach resonate more deeply with clients, increasing engagement and fostering loyalty.

Collaboration Enhances Outcomes

• Regular communication and collaboration with the client uncovered hidden opportunities and strengthen partnerships.

By focusing on the client’s evolving needs and creating personalized, multichannel engagement campaigns, the company not only recovered lost revenue but also solidified its position as a trusted partner. This approach demonstrates a scalable and effective model to drive client retention, expand existing accounts, and ensure sustained growth.
